Classic Margherita Pizza

Learn how to make the classic Margherita pizza at home with fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil. Follow this simple recipe for a delicious homemade pizza experience.

Get the Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 200g fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 2 large tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tomato sauce (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 250°C (482°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough onto a pizza stone or baking tray.
  4. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce (optional) over the dough.
  5. Arrange the mozzarella slices evenly on the pizza.
  6. Top with tomato slices, basil leaves, salt, and pepper.
  7. Drizzle with olive oil.
  8. Bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven and top with extra basil leaves for garnish.
  10. Slice, serve, and enjoy your homemade Margherita pizza!
