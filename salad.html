Healthy Green Salad

Learn how to make a Healthy Green Salad, a light and refreshing dish with fresh vegetables, avocado, and a tangy lemon dressing. Follow this simple recipe for a healthy and tasty experience.

Get the Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed lettuce (Romaine, Arugula, Spinach)
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled (optional)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the mixed lettuce, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and avocado.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, honey (optional), salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss everything gently to combine.
  4. Top with crumbled feta cheese (optional) and serve immediately. Enjoy!
