Chicken Tikka Masala

A flavorful Indian dish featuring grilled chicken in a creamy, spiced tomato sauce.

Get the Recipe

Ingredients

For the Chicken Marinade:

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil (for grilling)

For the Masala Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, and spices for the marinade.
  2. Add chicken cubes and coat well. Marinate for at least 1 hour (or overnight for better flavor).
  3. Grill or pan-fry the marinated chicken until cooked and slightly charred. Set aside.
  4. In a pan, heat butter and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute until fragrant.
  6. Stir in the cumin, coriander, garam masala, and chili powder.
  7. Add tomato puree and simmer for 10 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  8. Pour in heavy cream, mix well, and cook for another 5 minutes.
  9. Add the grilled chicken to the sauce and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  10. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan.
