Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

A rich and hearty Italian pasta dish with a slow-cooked meat sauce.

Get the Recipe

Ingredients

For the Bolognese Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, finely diced
  • 1 celery stalk, finely diced
  • 500g ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1 bay leaf

For the Pasta:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • Salt for boiling water

For Garnish:

  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onion, carrot, and celery. Saut√© until soft.
  2. Add garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the ground beef, breaking it apart with a spoon. Cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomato paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Pour in red wine (if using) and let it simmer until mostly evaporated.
  6. Add crushed tomatoes, beef broth, oregano, basil, chili flakes, bay leaf, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  7. Reduce heat to low and let simmer for at least 30 minutes (preferably 1-2 hours for deeper flavor), stirring occasionally.
  8. Stir in milk and cook for another 10 minutes. Remove the bay leaf.
  9. Meanwhile, cook spaghetti according to package instructions in salted boiling water until al dente.
  10. Drain the pasta and toss it with the Bolognese sauce.
  11. Serve hot, garnished with Parmesan cheese and fresh basil.
